WINFO's 7th Annual Hackathon

Every year, we bring together students with diverse skill sets to develop solutions that address a wide array of issues. Our theme for this year was A World of Difference. In the iSchool community, we believe in designing and developing technology based solutions that positively impact the world. This year, we challenged over 150 participants to venture into a new problem space and develop thoughtful solutions that empower those around us to promote equity.

WINFO's 6th Annual Hackathon

WINFO’s 6th annual hackathon was a one-day event open to ALL students at the University of Washington. This year, the theme of this year’s hackathon was, “What does diversity mean to you?” About 200 students brought their best ideas forward and worked in groups to solve this challenge together. 

A Big Thanks to this year's Sponsors!

WINFO's 5th Annual Hackathon

WINFO’s 5th annual hackathon was a full-day event open to all majors and students at the University of Washington. With 150 attendees, this was some of the highest attendance yet. We were lucky to represent companies such as Alaska Airlines, Avanade, G.E. Digital, Nordstrom, and many more.

WINFO's 4th Annual Hackathon

WINFO’s 4th annual hackathon was designed to empower young women leaders in technical fields. By providing a fun and safe environment in which to explore computing, the hackathon encourages and supports university women to become producers of future innovations in technology and help solve challenges in the world today.
